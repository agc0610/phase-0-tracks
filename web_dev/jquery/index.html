What Does a Balanced Meal Look Like?

Teach your child about putting together a balanced meal! Help them choose one food from each category, then click and drag it to its matching color section of the plate. As you build your plate you will learn a little about the foods.

To delete a food from your plate, double-click on it. To clear your plate, click the "start over" button.
